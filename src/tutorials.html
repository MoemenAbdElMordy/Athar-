For people of determination

Getting started with Athar

A complete introduction to using the Athar platform, from creating your account to finding your first accessible location.

Welcome to Athar! This comprehensive guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started with our platform.

Creating your account

Download the Athar app from the App Store or Google Play. Tap "Sign Up" and enter your email address or connect with your preferred social account.

Setting up your profile

Complete your profile by adding information about your accessibility needs. This helps us personalize your experience and show you the most relevant information.

Finding accessible locations

Use the search function to find locations near you. Filter by accessibility features like wheelchair access, quiet spaces, or accessible parking.

Understanding accessibility ratings

Learn how to read and interpret accessibility information for different types of locations.

Athar uses a comprehensive rating system to help you understand the accessibility features of any location.

Rating categories

We rate locations across multiple dimensions: mobility access, visual accessibility, hearing accessibility, and sensory-friendly features.

Understanding the symbols

Green checkmarks indicate fully accessible features. Orange symbols indicate partial accessibility. Red indicators show barriers or inaccessible features.

For volunteers and companions

Disability etiquette and communication

Learn respectful, person-first communication and avoid common mistakes when offering assistance.

Respectful communication is the foundation of good volunteer support. Here are key principles to follow.

Person-first language

Say "person with a disability" rather than "disabled person." Focus on the individual, not their disability. Avoid patronizing language.

Always ask before helping

Never assume someone needs assistance. Always ask "May I help you?" and wait for their response. If they decline, respect that decision.

Safety and boundaries

Maintaining appropriate boundaries, personal safety, and professional conduct as a volunteer.

Maintaining clear boundaries ensures safe, positive experiences for everyone involved.

Professional boundaries

Keep your relationship within the volunteer context. Don't ask for personal contact information outside the app. Focus on the person's needs.

Personal safety

Meet in public places. Share your location with a trusted contact using the in-app feature. Trust your instincts.
