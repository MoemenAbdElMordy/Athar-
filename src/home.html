Building a more accessible world

Athar is dedicated to breaking down barriers and creating inclusive environments. We believe everyone deserves the freedom to explore, learn, and connect — regardless of their abilities. Through technology and community, we're making accessibility a reality.

Who is Athar for?

People of Determination

Navigate the world with confidence. Access detailed information and connect with volunteers when you need assistance.

Volunteers

Make a meaningful difference. Offer support, guidance, and companionship to those who need it most in your community.

Businesses

Showcase your commitment. Help us build a comprehensive directory of accessible locations and inclusive services.

How Athar works

Athar provides a comprehensive ecosystem of accessibility resources and community support through our mobile platform and web tutorials.

1

Learn accessibility

Access comprehensive tutorials and guides about accessibility, assistive technologies, and inclusive practices for both users and volunteers.

2

Explore accessible places

Discover restaurants, shops, and public spaces with detailed, community-verified accessibility information. Know before you go.

3

Connect with support

Request assistance from trained volunteers. Whether you need navigation help or companionship, our community is here for you.
